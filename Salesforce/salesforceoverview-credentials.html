    In Salesforce, the "Rating" field on the Account object is a standard field used to classify the quality or priority of a potential or existing customer. While Salesforce provides default values, organizations often customize this field or add additional custom rating types to suit their specific business needs. -Default Rating Field (Standard Salesforce Setup) By default, the Rating picklist on the Account object typically includes these values: Hot High-priority account; likely to buy soon or very valuable. Warm - Medium-priority; shows interest but needs nurturing. Cold - Low-priority; little or no engagement currently. These are usually used in lead qualification, account scoring, or sales prioritization
