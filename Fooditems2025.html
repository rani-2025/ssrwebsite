Gongura

1. gongura kadigi make dry 1. add bit more oil , jira, avalu, chana, minapappu, velluli danchi ,red chiilies (3 to 4), mirchi cut into half, add salt after fried keep in same pan 2. add gongura dont add water and make it cook well

pudina

1. pudina kadigi make dry 1. red chillies (as per pudina), daniyalu, chenaga pappu, minappapu, jira, little oil (no avalau) fry and takeout from pan and add add garlic (5 to 6) raw keep in mixi and add little water 2. fry pudina well then only add chinta pandu wash and take out water (little) then fry it then add this into mixi jar along with step 2 powder 3. then add popu (more oil, jira, avalu, manappau, if not enough karam u can add red chillies)
