Limitation of On-Premise Systems

On-premise systems are often limited by hardware, scalability, and high maintenance costs. With the advent of cloud computing, these limitations can be mitigated. Some key areas to note:

  • Slow Data Recovery: Data recovery times are typically slower on on-premise systems compared to cloud systems that offer faster disaster recovery and redundancy features.
  • Faster Data Recovery: In the cloud, data can be restored much faster, thanks to distributed systems and backup services.
